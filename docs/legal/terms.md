# NexLog Usage Terms

NexLog is provided for authorized defensive security, incident response,
forensic training, CTF/lab analysis, and blue-team investigations.

Do not use NexLog for unauthorized access, unlawful monitoring, or activity
that violates applicable laws or policies.

NexLog findings are analyst aids, not legal conclusions. Validate detections,
timestamps, evidence integrity, and reports before relying on them in a real
investigation.

The software is provided as-is under the project license without warranty.
